Davy Jones was an English singer-songwriter, musician, actor and businessman, best known as a member of the band the Monkees, and for starring in the TV series of the same name.

Peter Tork was an English singer-songwriter, musician, actor and businessman, best known as a member of the band the Monkees, and for starring in the TV series of the same name.

Micky Dolenz is an American actor, musician, television director, radio personality and theater director, best known as a vocalist and drummer of the 1960s pop/rock band the Monkees.

Michael Nesmith is an American musician, songwriter, actor, producer, novelist, businessman, and philanthropist, best known as a member of the pop rock band the Monkees.

Davy Jones was born in Manchester. at the age of 21 from 1966 to 1971, Jones became a member of The Monkees and was the only Monkee to sign a deal with the studio. He sang lead vocals on many of the Monkees' recordings, including "I Wanna Be Free" and "Daydream Believer". The ever talented Jones was also a good drummer.

Peter Tork was born in Washington, D.C. in 1942. Tork was the oldest member of the group and was a proficient musician, and though the group was not allowed to play their own instruments on their first two albums, he was an exception, playing what he described as "third chair guitar" on Mike Nesmith's song, "Papa Gene's Blues," from their first album

Micky Dolenz had his own group called "Micky and the One-Nighters" with himself as lead singer. His band's live stage act included rock songs, cover songs, and even R&B. His favourite was the classic Chuck Berry's "Johnny B. Goode", the song Dolenz chose to sing for his first Monkees audition. He taught himself to play the drums and loved playing with passion.

Michael Nesmith was originally born in Houston. As he grew, his passion for music led him to land the role as the wool-hat-wearing guitar player "Mike" in the show, which required real-life musical talent (writing, instrument playing, singing, recording, and performing in live concerts as part of The Monkees musical band). Mike is an excellent musician.
